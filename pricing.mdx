## How Fees Are Deducted

Fees are **automatically deducted** during settlement:

```mermaid
graph LR
    A[Payment: $1,000] --> B[Verification: Free]
    B --> C[Settlement Initiated]
    C --> D[Fee Deducted: $5]
    D --> E[You Receive: $995]

    style B fill:#d4edda
    style D fill:#fff3cd
    style E fill:#d4edda
```

The settlement process:

1. Customer pays $1,000
2. You verify (free)
3. You initiate settlement
4. We deduct 0.5% ($5)
5. You receive $995
